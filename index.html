Why Choose yourSpace?

The answer is simple; yourSpace is meant for you, and it's constantly evolving to fit your needs. yourSpace is a way to organize your busy life, through a easy-to-use interface and through effective tools you can use to maximize productivity. Within these tools, there is a "todo list", where you can plan the tasks you need to accomplish. Through a minimalistic interface which gives you a feeling of satisfaction every time you complete a task, you will be kept on track and committed to completing your goals! There is also a "productivity mode", where you can effectively block out any websites that might be a distraction to you. There's also a space where you can self-meditate through controlled breathing or through relaxing music. And, whenever you open a new tab, a motivational quote, with a variety of different backgrounds, will keep you motivated throughout your daily tasks. yourSpace isn't just a way to be more productive, it's a way to gain inner peace and be the amazing person you were meant to be .

Future Updates (v1.2.0 and v1.3.0)

v1.2.0

In v1.2.0, yourSpace will add more usability to the "todo list", allowing users to change the order of their already added lists, and goals within the lists themselves. A "breaking news" section will also be added to the new tab page, alongside a weather tab that has more information, including a 7-day forecast.

v1.3.0

In v.1.3.0, the yourSpace team will be finished with the yourSpace forum, which is a online discussion forum that allows users to discuss with others who also want to improve their lives, in every aspect possible. There will also be a plethora of articles posted here, to encourage growth and a positive growth mindset.
